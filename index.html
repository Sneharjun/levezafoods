ABOUT US

Leveza Foods - Taste the Freshness, the Easier Way

Great food isn’t just about taste; it’s about trust. At Leveza Foods, we prioritize trust and convenience in frozen foods. From humble beginnings as a small poultry distribution in 2008, we've grown into South India's largest poultry and food solutions provider, serving homes, restaurants, and retailers. With a commitment to quality and convenience, we've gained the trust of retail giants like Lulu Group India and established a strong presence in the HoReCa segment. As we continue to grow, our promise remains the same: to serve great food, made simple, and to be a symbol of trust, comfort, and happiness with every bite.

OUR SERVICES

IMPORT/EXPORT

Leveza Foods sources the finest ingredients from worldwide locations to deliver premium-quality products to customers. We transport ethnic spices as well as superior cuts of meat together with international flavours to meet customers where they live.

WHOLESALE

The wholesale section of our services enables restaurants, hotels, and retail outlets to access fresh frozen bulk product offerings at cost-effective rates. The service provides ideal conditions for companies that wish to buy multiple quality ingredients

DISTRIBUTION

Fresh frozen goods are delivered to South India via our well-established customer delivery system. We maintain both our reefer vehicle lineup and our warehouses in strategic locations, which allow for efficient and timely service delivery.

RETAIL

Our retail line, which includes juicy chicken cuts and ready-to-cook items that are accessible at distribution centres, is distinguished by its everyday convenience. Our stores provide the chance for you to discover our product offerings so you can experience authentic quality flavours at home.
